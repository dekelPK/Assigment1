5 health benefits of blueberries :

  1. Reduce the chance of heart disease -
    From a study published in 2019 in the American Journal of Clinical Nutrition, it appears that consuming blueberries daily can reduce the risk of heart disease and even improve the condition of patients. The researchers tested the effect of eating blueberries on about 140 participants, who suffer from excess weight and metabolic syndrome (a collection of risk factors that predict heart disease and diabetes - such as hypertension, cholesterol and blood sugar). They found that eating a cup of blueberries a day (about 150 grams), for at least six months - can significantly improve blood vessel function and reduce the risk of heart disease by 12-15 percent. Although this is a small study, funded by agricultural associations - but the results show that blueberries definitely have quite a bit of health potential.
  2. . High nutritional value and low caloric value -
    Blueberries are rich in vitamins, minerals, dietary fiber and antioxidants - and all this with a relatively low caloric value, making them an excellent snack for people who are watching their weight. Blueberries contain, among other things, potassium, quite a bit of vitamin K, vitamin C and folic acid - and only 85 calories.
  3. Balance the cholesterol in the blood -
    Blueberries can reduce LDL cholesterol levels - the "bad cholesterol" that can lead to artery clogging and cardiovascular disease. Blueberries contain phytosterols, compounds previously found to be able to reduce LDL cholesterol levels in healthy adults by 8-10 percent.
  4. Rich in antioxidants -
    Antioxidants are important for preventing chronic diseases, as well as contributing to reducing DNA damage and thus preventing accelerated aging of the body's cells. They are especially rich in the antioxidant anthocyanin, a pigment that can reduce the risk of cancer and chronic diseases. They also contain pterostilbene, another antioxidant found to be able to prevent breast cancer and heart disease.
  5. Good for the digestive system -
    Blueberries have prebiotic components, which can improve the function of the digestive system and prevent intestinal diseases. Prebiotics are actually the food of the probiotic bacteria, that is, the friendly bacteria in our digestive system that are important, among other things, for the effective absorption of nutrients, including vitamins and minerals. In addition to this, since prebiotics are food for the friendly bacteria, they contribute to the balance of the bacteria in the digestive system and thus prevent an increase in the amount of "bad" bacteria that can lead to the development of various diseases in the digestive system.

A wonderful recipe for a hearty cake with fresh blueberries :

The ingredients (for a round mold with a diameter of 24) -

  • 1/2 cup brown sugar
  • 3 eggs
  • 150 ml of oil
  • 130 grams of fresh blueberries
  • 220 grams of flour
  • 6 grams of baking powder
  • 6 grams of baking soda
  • 1 teaspoon of cinnamon
  • Pinch of salt
  • 50 grams of chopped nuts
Preparation:
Grease the pan and sprinkle some flour on the sides.
Heat the oven to 170 degrees. In a mixer with a whisk attachment, beat eggs and sugar until a stable foam is obtained.
Lower the speed of the mixer and pour in the oil while mixing until a uniform mixture is obtained.
Sift the dry ingredients and fold with a spatula.
Add the blueberries and fold lightly.
Pour into a mold and bake for about 35-40 minutes.

Blueberry Varieties:

Eliot

A good commercial variety with a relatively large fruit. The Eliot is a variety that is very suitable for the end of the season. The fruit has a delicate sweetness and a sour taste.

Biloxi

A classic southern variety. Does not go well with cold. It is recommended for growing in hot areas, from the strongest and most resistant varieties that exist. High yield therefore loved by farmers.

legacy

A strong and very resistant southern strain can grow all over the country. A bush that yields large quantities and is therefore very suitable for commercial cultivation and very popular with farmers.

climax

One of the most popular rabbit eye varieties. Resistant to extreme heat and can also happen multiple times and temperatures below 0. Begins to bloom as soon as winter ends.

Jersey

The growth rate of the Jersey is one of the highest among the other varieties, developing to the height and sides, strong and stable branches. The fruit yield is high.

Brigita

Australian development, a very special breed. The fruits are large and tasty, the texture is particularly crispy and their shelf life is long compared to the other varieties. A large, strong and durable bush.

Diok

A variety that produces a large and sweet fruit. Has a relatively long shelf life than other varieties. High fruit yield and therefore loved by farmers. Suitable for growing from an altitude of 650 meters.

Pink lemonade

A variety with very sweet pink fruits that have a unique taste. Suitable for growing in most parts of the country. An upright and impressive shrub with elongated leaves.
